The Electric State (2025)

Director: Russo Brothers

Starring: Millie Bobby Brown, Chris Pratt

Genre: sci-fi

The Electric State, directed by the Russo Brothers and set to release on March 14, 2025, is a retro-futuristic sci-fi adventure based on Simon Stålenhag’s graphic novel. The film stars Millie Bobby Brown as Michelle, a young girl searching for her brother in a dystopian America where robots and AI have gone rogue. Accompanied by a toy robot and a drifter (Chris Pratt), Michelle uncovers a deeper mystery in a world scarred by an android rebellion that has left society disconnected.

Visually, The Electric State incorporates the aesthetic of the 1990s with a gritty, surreal twist, featuring abandoned robots, desert landscapes, and eccentric characters like a mascot resembling Mr. Peanut. The film promises a blend of high-stakes action with emotional depth, much like the Russo Brothers’ Marvel work, as they aim to reimagine Stålenhag’s world rather than replicate it exactly.

The adaptation has sparked some fan debate, as the directors modified the story to fit a cinematic structure, shifting from a quiet existential journey to a faster-paced plot. If you're into atmospheric sci-fi with a mix of adventure and visual nostalgia, this film could be an exciting watch!
